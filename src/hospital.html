Hospital Employment

This visualization explores how hospital employment varies across countries, reflecting the dynamics of healthcare systems worldwide. By examining the workforce, including roles such as physicians, nurses, and healthcare assistants, we can gain insights into how nations allocate resources to meet public health demands.

The world map allows you to interactively select a country and see its workforce distribution. Clicking on a country reveals the grouped bar chart and the pie chart of the specific country

Grouped Barchart: Displays changes over time in various employment categories, such as professional nurses and other staff.

Pie Chart: Illustrates the current composition of the workforce, highlighting the proportion of different roles.
